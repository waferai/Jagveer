About Dr. Jagveer Singh

Senior Consultant - Pediatric Neurology

Home / About

Pediatric Neurologist in Jaipur

Dr. Jagveer Singh is a Pediatric Neurologist based in Jaipur. He is a passionate and skilled pediatric neurologist with good clinical experience in diagnosing and managing neurological and developmental disorders in infants, children, and adolescents.

He has expertise in epilepsy, neurodevelopmental conditions, and neuro-rehabilitation, with a strong academic background and several publications. He is known for delivering compassionate and evidence-based care.

With over a decade of rich experience and specialized training, he has emerged as a trusted name in child neurology across the region.

Dr. Jagveer Singh has developed this website to educate and make parents/caregivers aware about common pediatric neurological/behavioral issues in their understandable language (in Hindi) in a layman manner.
